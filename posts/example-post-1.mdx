### Additional Note:
- Some sites, including search engines like Google and DuckDuckGo, have **X-Frame-Options** enabled, which prevent them from being embedded in an iframe. Therefore, it's recommended to link to them directly.
- **Other search engines** that may work include **StartPage** or **Qwant**, which are also privacy-focused.
